Welcome to SegaRasa

SegaRasa is a portal for purchasing fresh and high-quality fruits and vegetables. All our produce is grown by farmers across Indonesia who ensure the freshness and quality of their products.


Our goal is to be a convenient and user-friendly platform for food shopping while maintaining the highest level of service quality. We aim to assist our customers in shopping efficiently, allowing them to focus on other important matters in their daily lives.
